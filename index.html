Wildlife Refuge

Technology | Firebase, GCP, Python, fast.ai, React.js, JavaScript

In this project, I collaborated with the Westin Reserva Conchal from Costa Rica and developed a Web App to revolutionize wildlife monitoring and visitor experience at Reserva Conchal's 98-acre Wildlife Refuge. Instead of writing down sighting records while going on the trail every day, employees can now use our solution to document animal activities more efficiently. Our solution also includes an animal recognition feature for visitors to enhance the tourism experience while introducing crowdsourcing data.

Multi-task Model of House Pricing

Technology | Python, PyTorch Lightning, Optuna

This study adopted PyTorch Lightning to obtain multiple targets using the shared layers. The regression target in this study will be the sale prices of houses, and the classification target is the categories they belong to. Several combinations of activation functions, optimizers, and loss functions are included in the experiments. Last but not least, hyperparameter tuning is performed on the depth and size of this neural network model.

Airline Satisfaction

Technology | Python, Causal ML

In this study, I worked in a team of 7 people to investigate satisfaction level of airline passengers. I was responsible for the causal inference section, studying the impact of inflight online services and online boarding availability. S, T, and X learners were implemented, and CATE (Conditional Average Treatment Effect) was studied for both treatments. Visualization including feature importance, SHAP value, and 3D treatment effect plot were presented as well.

News Platform Topic Modeling

Technology | Python, BeautifulSoup, Gensim and NLTK

Every day, thousands of articles are written by news platforms based on their interests, reader preferences,trending topics, and political inclinations among other factors. The aim of this project is to collect news articles from different competing platforms and understand the topics they write about the most. The analysis can help multiple stakeholders including marketers who are selecting platforms, individuals who are searching information that draws their interest, and technology companies who are building their recommendation engines.

Communauto Station Re-Allocation

Technology | Python, Gurobi Optimization, Folium for Visualization

A mixed integer model is formulated to derive the optimal solution for a relocation problem using Gurobi optimization. Communauto is a company providing sharing-car service. One of its option is called "Round-Trip", that is users have to pick up cars from a certain location and return them back to exactly the same place after using. This study focuses on the Round-Trip service and evaluate the best relocation solution if Communauto decided to adopt parking-lot-based policy instead of street-parking-based.

Shark Tank Viewership Study

Technology | R, gbm, random forest, boost, dplyr

This study aimed to classify whether the participants in the tv show Shark Tank will succeed and study the relationship between features and viewership. It shed light on the success probability of each entrepreneur as the production team would want to balance the number of deals in each episode. Furthermore, the potential relationships between viewership and other variables will be examined for audience retention purposes.
