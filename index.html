About

Hi, I am Ying-Fang, a dedicated enthusiast in rationalizing real-world challenges and extracting valuable insights using data analytics methodologies. I have a 3-year of work experience in KPI improvement and troubleshooting production line issues; 1-year experience in data analytics consulting in the aerospace and sustainability industries. Drawing upon my comprehensive background in engineering and business, encompassing both professional and academic achievements, I am poised to embark on my next promising venture.


During my leisure moments, I enjoy hiking and appreciate each trail's breathtaking landscapes. Cooking is one of my hobbies too. I find it satisfying to see the rising dough, to be surrounded by delightful aromas, and to catch the smiles on the faces of my cherished ones. Please don't hesitate to share your favourite trail or dish with me!
